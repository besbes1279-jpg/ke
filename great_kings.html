Kings of Ancient Egypt

Hatshepsut

The powerful queen who built Deir el-Bahari temple and expanded Egypt’s trade routes.

Ramses II

One of the most powerful pharaohs.

Tutankhamun

Famous for his intact tomb.

Khufu

Builder of the Great Pyramid.

Cleopatra VII

The last pharaoh of Egypt, known for her intelligence, political alliances, and dramatic life story with Rome.
